About Me

Hello, my name is Zachary Huff, I am a graphic designer who has a passion for creating. My areas of expertise include video production/editing, photography, and illustration. My interest in graphic design developed when I began experimenting with video editing software, creating video content and posting it to YouTube. After several years of this, I developed an interest in illustration and vector design, designing logos, maps, book covers, and so much more. My interest in photography developed in 2021 where I began taking photos of anything and everything I found interesting. I now use these skills to create imagery that entertains and affects people positively.
